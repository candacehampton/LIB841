God

Growing up, I absolutely loved staying with my grandmother (we called her granny). Every Saturday night I would stay with her and every Sunday morning we got dressed and went to church. I loved Sunday school and I have always felt peace at church. At the age of 11, I was saved and baptized. I can't imagine a life where God is not the center and Jesus as my Savior. Now I take my little girl to church and my heart jumps when she gets up and sings songs of praise to our Lord!

Family

God first, then family. This makes sense to me since I wouldn't have my family, without God. Where I am from, family is important. I had an amazing childhood with two parents who love me and now love their grandchildren. I am close to my siblings and their families. Most of my family lives nearby so we see each other often and are always availble to take care of one another.

Work

As mentioned on my home page, I have worked in education for 15 years. I started in Kindergarten, switched to special education and now I am in the Library Media Specialist position. I have my dual certification in Elementary Education P-5 and Learning and Behavior Disorders P-12. I have my Master in Teacher Leader: Special Education. I am now working under a provisional certificate for LMS which means I am taking the classes while doing the job. I have learned so much in my Library classes and I'm excited to continue next year, improving upon and implementing my new knowledge. My school babies deserve the best.
